I was reading this great HN thread and people asked about linux-friendly Ableton. As macOS is very just about dead to me I really want something that I can use on Linux/Windows as well. Had previously been playing around with Ardour and Repear, but have always missed the layout/workflow of Ableton. A bit simpler and better-suited for assembling things out of loops and samples.

Downloaded Bitwig Studio (~230MB) and installed to have a play. This is how you do demo software! Fully featured, everything works except save/export. For my early trials this is perfect as I'm basically at the level of doing covers of Human Music.

But playing around with The Grid and some of the other built-in stuff this almost definitely feels like a buy.
